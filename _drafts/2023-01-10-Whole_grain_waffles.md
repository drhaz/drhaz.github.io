Ingredients
===


 * 70 g butter
 * 30 g sugar, or half and half sugar / artificial sweetener

 * 2 eggs
 * juice of 1/2 lemon
 * 3 table spoon rum
 * 90 ml milk
 
 * 120 g whole wheat flour
 * 1 tea spoon baking powder
